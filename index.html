Card Guide

20 HP to 100 HP Pokemon cards count as 2 through 10.
Energy cards are Aces and can count as 1 or 11.
Trainer cards are your Jacks, Queens, and Kings.
The game uses a 6-deck shoe for a fast, casino-style flow.
