How To Play

You always start with the Japanese Pack locked in.
Win hands in a row to unlock bigger prizes.
Matching opening pairs can split once. Win both split hands to jump two levels.
Any opening blackjack locks an extra coupon for the run.
If you lose before cashing out, you fall back to the starter prize. Pushes replay the same level.

Card Guide

20 HP to 100 HP Pokemon cards count as 2 through 10.
Energy cards are Aces and can count as 1 or 11.

Matching opening pairs can split once. Opening blackjacks lock bonus coupons.
